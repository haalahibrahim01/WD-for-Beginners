Calculate your BMR

Posted on November 08, 2022

BMR stands for Basal Metabolic Rate. It is the calories you burn even when resting through basic life-sustaining functions like breathing, circulation, nutrient processing, and cell production. By using various predetermined formulas based on an individuals body measurements and activity level, his/her BMR can be roughly estimated. Head on to this page to calculate your BMR.
